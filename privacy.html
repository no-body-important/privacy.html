Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Assistant Messaging Service (a U.S.-based sole proprietor service) collects, uses, and shares information when providing an AI phone assistant and SMS follow-up messaging service for participating businesses (the “Service”).

Quick summary: If you call or text a participating business, we may process your phone number, call details, message content, and inquiry details so the business can respond. We do not sell personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information, depending on your interaction with the Service:

  • Phone numbers and contact details: phone number and any contact details you provide during calls or messages (such as your name or email address).
  • Call details and metadata: date/time, duration, routing/transfer events, recording status (if enabled), and similar call-related information.
  • Message content and metadata: SMS/MMS content, delivery status, timestamps, and conversation thread history.
  • Interaction history: prior messages, conversation context, and AI-generated summaries related to your inquiry.
  • Customer inquiry details: information you share about your request (for example, appointment needs, service questions, or support issues).

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate the Service and respond to customer inquiries, including answering calls, collecting inquiry details, and routing messages.
  • Notify the relevant business and its authorized personnel about customer requests and provide summaries to help them respond.
  • Send service-related follow-up messages connected to a customer-initiated interaction (for example, appointment-related messages and support replies).
  • Improve reliability and performance (for example, debugging call flow issues and reducing delivery errors).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

3. SMS and Calling Communications

The Service may send conversational, service-related SMS messages after you contact a participating business. Message frequency varies based on your interaction and the business workflow.

  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe from further messages where supported.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance where supported.
  • Carrier fees: Standard message and data rates may apply.

If you opt out, you may no longer receive SMS follow-ups through this Service for that conversation.

4. When Information May Be Shared

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With the business you contacted: If you contact a participating business, relevant information may be shared with that business or its authorized personnel to respond to your inquiry or request.
  • With third-party providers: We may use third-party providers (such as communications/telephony, hosting, analytics, or AI service providers) to support the Service. These providers process information on our behalf to deliver the Service.
  • For legal, security, and safety reasons: We may share information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the Service.

We do not sell personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, security, compliance, dispute resolution, and operational purposes. Some data may be deleted sooner, and some may be retained longer when required.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices and Opt-Out Rights
